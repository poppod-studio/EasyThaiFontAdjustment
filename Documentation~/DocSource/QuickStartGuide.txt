EASY THAI FONT ADJUSTMENT - QUICK START GUIDE
=============================================
By Poppod Studio

WHAT IT DOES
------------
Fixes floating Thai vowels and tone marks in TextMeshPro font assets by
generating Glyph Pair Adjustment Records (kerning) automatically.


STEP 1 - OPEN THE TOOL
----------------------
   Tools > Easy Thai Font Adjustment

   (Or right-click a TMP_FontAsset > Easy Thai Font Adjustment)


STEP 2 - SELECT YOUR FONT
-------------------------
   Drag your TMP Font Asset into the "Font Asset" field.
   The tool reports how many Thai consonants the font covers.


STEP 3 - ONE-CLICK FIX
----------------------
   Click "Fix Everything Automatically".
   The tool generates all 522 Thai pair adjustments based on the
   font's metrics and writes them into the Font Asset.


STEP 4 - VERIFY
---------------
   Type Thai text with stacked vowels/tone marks in any TMP component.
   Vowels and tone marks should now sit correctly above the consonants.


UNDO
----
   Click "Undo Last Changes" inside the tool, or press Ctrl/Cmd + Z.
   A backup is taken automatically before every apply.

   - Templates Tab: adjust the 7 built-in presets.
